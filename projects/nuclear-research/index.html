Nuclear Testing and Research

Welcome to Marcel Nuclear Testing and Research, my Minecraft project on PuffCraft. Basically, it’s just two nuclear cooling towers with creeper farms inside.

(Honestly, I had some pretty dumb ideas a few years ago... LMAO)

Inspired by nuclear sites others built in Minecraft, I took the chance to be a “company” that explodes stacks of TNT in random spots as a nuclear testing site.

It was a pretty enjoyable server, if I’m being honest.
