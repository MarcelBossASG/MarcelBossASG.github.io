Nuclear Testing and Research

Welcome to Marcel Nuclear Testing and Research — a solo-built Minecraft project born on PuffCraft, where creativity met controlled chaos.

What started as experiments with TNT evolved into a full-blown fascination with nuclear aesthetics. Cooling towers, remote facilities, and precise setups came to life block by block — all imagined, designed, and constructed by one person.

Inspired by the look and feel of real-world nuclear sites, this project channels a passion for design, science, and the satisfaction of big explosions (in a safe Minecraft setting).

It’s not just about blowing stuff up — it’s about building something ambitious, detailed, and uniquely mine.

Established: 27/06/2023
